So you want to impress people?

Throwing a frisbee is the best way to impress people at any gathering.

Following these simple steps, you will be the most popular person anywhere you go.

Pick up the frisbee

Grip the disk like you are making a fist

Put your index finger on the rim for extra balance

Assume the Positon

Step across your body with your dominate foot

Keep the arm holding the disc around chest level across your body

Let it rip!

To make the frisbee fly straight act like you are whipping a towel

Release the disk at the midpoint of your body, while looking where you want the disk to go.

Look at it fly!

You did it!

Everyone is super impressed and wants to hang out. Congratulations!
